Domestic Destinations

Explore our handpicked destinations across India with special focus on Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," Rishikesh is a spiritual hub situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River. This picturesque city offers the perfect blend of spiritual enlightenment, adventure activities, and natural beauty.

Quick Facts

Uttarakhand
Best Time: September - April
Nearest Airport: Dehradun (35 km)
Languages: Hindi, Garhwali

Top attractions include the iconic suspension bridges of Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula, serene ashrams, evening Ganga Aarti ceremony at Triveni Ghat, and thrilling adventure sports like white water rafting and bungee jumping.

World-renowned yoga and meditation retreats
Thrilling adventure activities including white water rafting
Spiritual experiences along the sacred Ganges River

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Nestled in the Kumaon foothills of the Himalayas, Nainital is a charming hill station built around the emerald Naini Lake. Known as the "Lake District of India," it offers breathtaking mountain scenery, pleasant weather, and a variety of outdoor activities.

Quick Facts

Uttarakhand
Best Time: March - June & September - November
Nearest Airport: Pantnagar (65 km)
Languages: Hindi, Kumaoni

Key attractions include the beautiful Naini Lake for boating, Naina Devi Temple, Snow View Point, Mall Road for shopping, and nearby attractions like Bhimtal, Sattal, and Naukuchiatal lakes.

Scenic boat rides on the pristine Naini Lake
Panoramic mountain views from Snow View Point and Tiffin Top
Colonial charm along Mall Road with shopping and dining options
View Nainital Packages

Goa

India's smallest state is also one of its most popular tourist destinations. With its pristine beaches, Portuguese colonial architecture, and vibrant nightlife, Goa offers a unique blend of Indian and Western cultures.

Quick Facts

Western coastal state
Best Time: November - February
International Airport at Dabolim
Languages: Konkani, English

Goa is divided into North and South Goa, each offering different experiences. North Goa is known for its lively beaches like Baga and Calangute, while South Goa offers more serene and less crowded beaches like Palolem and Agonda.

Beautiful beaches with golden sands and water sports
Portuguese-influenced architecture and historic churches
Vibrant nightlife and seafood cuisine
View Goa Packages

Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala is famous for its emerald backwaters, lush hill stations, pristine beaches, and Ayurvedic traditions. Located on the Malabar Coast, it offers a serene escape into nature's paradise.

Quick Facts

Southwestern coastal state
Best Time: September - March
International Airports at Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, and Kozhikode
Language: Malayalam

Kerala's major attractions include the backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom, hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad, beaches of Kovalam and Varkala, and wildlife sanctuaries like Periyar and Silent Valley.

Serene backwater cruises on traditional houseboats
Lush tea plantations and spice gardens
Traditional Ayurvedic wellness treatments
View Kerala Packages

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

The former summer capital of British India, Shimla is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Himalayan foothills. With its colonial architecture, panoramic mountain views, and pleasant climate, it remains one of India's most popular mountain retreats.

Quick Facts

Capital of Himachal Pradesh
Best Time: March - June & October - December
Shimla Airport at Jubbarhatti (22 km)
Languages: Hindi, Pahari

Major attractions include the Ridge, Mall Road, Christ Church, Jakhu Temple, Viceregal Lodge, and the UNESCO World Heritage Kalka-Shimla toy train journey. Nearby destinations like Kufri offer winter sports and year-round activities.

Colonial-era architecture and heritage buildings
Scenic mountain vistas and pine-covered hills
Historic toy train ride through picturesque landscapes
View Shimla Packages

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Nestled in the Beas River Valley, Manali is a stunning hill station that combines natural beauty with adventure opportunities. With snow-capped mountains, lush forests, and gushing rivers, it's a paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Quick Facts

Himachal Pradesh
Best Time: October - June
Nearest Airport: Bhuntar (50 km)
Languages: Hindi, Pahari

Popular attractions include Solang Valley for adventure sports, Hadimba Temple, Rohtang Pass for stunning mountain views, Old Manali for its bohemian atmosphere, and Vashisht Hot Springs. The region is also famous for its apple orchards and Kullu Valley nearby.

Thrilling adventure sports including paragliding and skiing
Snow-covered mountain passes and stunning valley views
Blend of Himalayan culture and vibrant tourist atmosphere
View Manali Packages

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its distinctive terracotta-colored buildings, magnificent palaces, and rich cultural heritage. As the capital of Rajasthan, it forms an important part of India's popular Golden Triangle tourist circuit.

Quick Facts

Capital of Rajasthan
Best Time: October - March
International Airport
Languages: Hindi, Rajasthani

Top attractions include the magnificent Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), Jantar Mantar observatory, and the vibrant bazaars of the old city.

Magnificent palaces and forts showcasing Rajput architecture
Rich cultural experiences and traditional Rajasthani folk performances
Colorful markets selling textiles, jewelry, and handicrafts
View Jaipur Packages

International Destinations

Explore exciting international destinations with our curated travel experiences

Thailand

Known as the "Land of Smiles," Thailand offers a perfect blend of stunning beaches, ancient temples, vibrant cities, and delicious cuisine. It's a versatile destination that caters to all types of travelers, from luxury seekers to budget backpackers.

Quick Facts

Southeast Asia
Best Time: November - February
International Airports at Bangkok, Phuket, etc.
Language: Thai (English widely spoken in tourist areas)

Major attractions include Bangkok's Grand Palace and floating markets, the historic city of Ayutthaya, pristine beaches of Phuket, Krabi, and Koh Samui, cultural experiences in Chiang Mai, and natural wonders like Phi Phi Islands and Khao Sok National Park.

Pristine beaches and tropical island paradises
Ancient temples and rich cultural heritage
World-famous cuisine and vibrant street food scene
View Thailand Packages

Dubai, UAE

A city of superlatives, Dubai is known for its ultra-modern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife. This futuristic city rising from the Arabian desert has transformed from a humble fishing village to a global metropolis in just a few decades.

Quick Facts

United Arab Emirates
Best Time: November - April
Dubai International Airport
Languages: Arabic, English widely spoken

Must-visit attractions include the Burj Khalifa (world's tallest building), Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Mall with its massive aquarium, desert safari experiences, Burj Al Arab, Dubai Marina, and the historic Al Fahidi district to glimpse Dubai's past.

Record-breaking architecture and engineering marvels
Luxury shopping, dining, and accommodation experiences
Desert adventures and cultural experiences
View Dubai Packages

Australia

Australia offers an incredible diversity of experiences - from iconic cities and world-class beaches to ancient rainforests and the vast outback. This island continent combines natural wonders, unique wildlife, and a relaxed, multicultural atmosphere.

Quick Facts

Oceania
Best Time: September - November & March - May
International Airports in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, etc.
Language: English

Top attractions include Sydney's Opera House and Harbor Bridge, the Great Barrier Reef, Melbourne's cultural scene, the Twelve Apostles along the Great Ocean Road, Uluru (Ayers Rock), the Daintree Rainforest, and wildlife experiences with koalas, kangaroos, and more.

Stunning natural landscapes and unique wildlife
Iconic cities with world-class dining and cultural experiences
Beautiful beaches and outdoor adventure opportunities
