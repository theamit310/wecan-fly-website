Rishikesh Yoga & Adventure

3 Days / 2 Nights
Rishikesh, Uttarakhand
Bestseller

Experience the perfect blend of spiritual enlightenment and adventure in the Yoga Capital of the World. Enjoy yoga sessions by the Ganges, thrilling white water rafting, and visits to sacred temples.

*Price may vary based on travel dates and accommodation type

Itinerary
Inclusions & Exclusions
Accommodation
1
Arrival in Delhi

Arrive at Delhi International Airport. Our representative will meet and assist you at the airport and transfer you to your hotel. After check-in, relax and recover from jet lag. Overnight stay in Delhi.

2
Delhi Sightseeing

After breakfast, proceed for a full-day sightseeing tour of Old and New Delhi. Visit historical monuments like Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb, and drive past government buildings. Evening free for shopping at Chandni Chowk. Overnight stay in Delhi.

3
Delhi to Agra

After breakfast, drive to Agra (approx. 4 hours). On arrival, check-in at your hotel. Later, visit the magnificent Agra Fort and the breathtaking Taj Mahal at sunset. Overnight stay in Agra.

4
Agra to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri

Early morning, visit Taj Mahal at sunrise (optional). After breakfast, drive to Jaipur (approx. 5 hours) with a stop at Fatehpur Sikri, the abandoned Mughal city. On arrival in Jaipur, check-in at your hotel. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

5
Jaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit Amber Fort with an elephant or jeep ride up the hill. Later, explore City Palace, Jantar Mantar Observatory, and drive past Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Afternoon, enjoy shopping in the local markets. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

6
Jaipur to Delhi Departure

After breakfast, drive back to Delhi (approx. 5 hours). Transfer to the airport for your onward journey with memories of your Golden Triangle tour.

Shimla-Manali Adventure

7 Days / 6 Nights
Kochi - Munnar - Thekkady - Alleppey
Premium

Experience the serene beauty of God's Own Country with this comprehensive Kerala tour covering hill stations, wildlife, and famous backwaters.

From ₹32,999 per person

*Price may vary based on travel dates and group size

Book Now
Itinerary
Inclusions & Exclusions
Accommodation
1
Arrival in Kochi

Arrive at Kochi International Airport. Our representative will meet and transfer you to your hotel. After check-in, visit major attractions like Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, and St. Francis Church. Evening, enjoy a Kathakali dance performance. Overnight in Kochi.

2
Kochi to Munnar

After breakfast, drive to Munnar (approx. 4 hours), a beautiful hill station famous for its tea plantations. En route, visit Valara and Cheeyappara waterfalls. On arrival, check-in at your hotel. Rest of the day at leisure to explore the surroundings. Overnight in Munnar.

3
Munnar Sightseeing

After breakfast, full-day sightseeing tour of Munnar. Visit Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Top Station for panoramic views. Explore the beautiful Eravikulam National Park (subject to opening) to see the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Overnight in Munnar.

4
Munnar to Thekkady

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady (approx. 3 hours), home to the famous Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. After check-in, visit a spice plantation to learn about various spices grown in Kerala. Evening, enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake to spot wildlife (subject to weather conditions). Overnight in Thekkady.

5
Thekkady to Alleppey

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey (approx. 4 hours), known for its backwaters. Check-in to a traditional houseboat and begin your cruise through the serene backwaters of Kerala. Enjoy lunch, dinner, and overnight stay on the houseboat.

6
Alleppey to Kochi

After breakfast on the houseboat, disembark and drive back to Kochi (approx. 2 hours). Check-in at your hotel. Rest of the day free for shopping and exploring the city at your own pace. Overnight in Kochi.

7
Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, transfer to Kochi International Airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories of Kerala.

Inclusions

  • 6 nights' accommodation (4-star hotels & premium houseboat)
  • Daily breakfast, 1 lunch and 1 dinner on houseboat
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle throughout the tour
  • Professional English-speaking tour guide
  • Kathakali dance show in Kochi
  • Spice plantation tour in Thekkady
  • Boat ride on Periyar Lake
  • All applicable taxes

Exclusions

  • Airfare
  • Meals other than mentioned
  • Personal expenses (laundry, telephone calls, etc.)
  • Camera fees at monuments
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Optional activities

Complete Uttarakhand Experience

7 Days / 6 Nights
Haridwar - Rishikesh - Mussoorie - Nainital
Our Specialty

Experience the best of Uttarakhand with our comprehensive tour package. From spiritual enlightenment in Haridwar and Rishikesh to the scenic beauty of Mussoorie and Nainital, discover the diverse landscapes of Dev Bhoomi.

From ₹19,999 per person

*Price may vary based on travel dates and group size

Book Now

Itinerary

1
Arrival in Haridwar

Arrive in Haridwar and check-in to your hotel. Visit Har Ki Pauri for the evening Ganga Aarti ceremony, a spiritual experience not to be missed. Return to your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

2
Haridwar to Rishikesh

After breakfast, drive to Rishikesh (25 km). Visit key attractions including Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, Triveni Ghat, and the Beatles Ashram. Evening free for leisure or optional yoga session. Overnight in Rishikesh.

3
Rishikesh Adventure Day

Full day dedicated to adventure activities in Rishikesh. Enjoy white water rafting on the Ganges (16km stretch), cliff jumping, and body surfing. Optional activities include bungee jumping or flying fox (additional cost). Evening at leisure. Overnight in Rishikesh.

4
Rishikesh to Mussoorie

After breakfast, drive to Mussoorie (80 km). Check-in at your hotel and later visit Gun Hill, Kempty Falls, and Mall Road. Enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayan ranges. Overnight in Mussoorie.

5
Mussoorie to Nainital

After breakfast, drive to Nainital (295 km). Check-in at your hotel upon arrival. Evening free to explore Mall Road and the lakeside area. Enjoy a boat ride on Naini Lake if time permits. Overnight in Nainital.

6
Nainital Lake Tour

Full day excursion to visit the Lake Tour (Bhimtal, Sattal, Naukuchiatal). Visit Snow View Point for panoramic views of the Himalayan peaks. Evening free for shopping at the local markets. Overnight in Nainital.

7
Departure

After breakfast, check-out from your hotel. Transfer to Kathgodam railway station or Pantnagar airport for your onward journey.

Inclusions

  • 6 nights' accommodation in 3-star hotels
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle for all transfers and sightseeing
  • Professional English-speaking tour guide
  • Yoga session in Rishikesh
  • White water rafting in Rishikesh (16km stretch)
  • All applicable taxes

Exclusions

  • Camera fees at monuments
