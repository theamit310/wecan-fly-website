About WeCan Fly

Our journey, mission, and commitment to creating unforgettable travel experiences in India

Our Story

Founded in 2020, WeCan Fly was born from a passion for showcasing the true essence of India. Our founders, avid travelers themselves, recognized the need for a tourism company that goes beyond the typical tourist experiences to provide authentic, immersive journeys across India's diverse landscapes and cultures.

What started as a small team with big dreams has now grown into a trusted name in India's tourism industry. We take pride in our deep knowledge of India's hidden gems, our sustainable travel practices, and our commitment to creating personalized experiences that leave lasting impressions.

Our name "WeCan Fly" represents our belief that travel lifts the human spirit, broadens horizons, and creates connections that transcend borders. We strive to give our clients the wings to explore India with freedom, comfort, and joy.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the authentic beauty, culture, and spirit of India while fostering sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve our natural and cultural heritage for future generations.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted tourism partner, known for our personalized service, insider knowledge, and commitment to responsible travel, making the diversity of India accessible and memorable for travelers from around the world.

Our Journey

2020

Humble Beginnings

WeCan Fly was founded with a vision to showcase India's diverse beauty. We started with just 3 team members and offered tours to 5 destinations.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched our first website and expanded our online presence. Introduced virtual tour experiences during the global travel restrictions.

2022

Growing Our Reach

Expanded our team to 15 members and added 10 new destinations across India. Partnered with premium hotels and local experience providers.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our "Travel Responsibly" initiative focusing on sustainable tourism practices and supporting local communities.

2024

Award Recognition

Recognized as one of India's Top 10 Emerging Tourism Companies. Expanded to serve international clients from 25+ countries.

2025

Today & Beyond

Continuing to innovate and expand our services, with a focus on authentic experiences and responsible tourism across all of India.
