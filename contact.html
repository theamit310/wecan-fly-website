Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, PayPal, and for Indian customers, we also accept UPI payments. For tour packages, we typically require a 25% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due 30 days before your trip begins.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For the best experience and availability, we recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for travel during peak tourist seasons (October-March). However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings whenever possible, subject to availability of accommodations and services.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy offers:
- Full refund minus processing fee for cancellations 30+ days before the trip
- 75% refund for cancellations 21-30 days before the trip
- 50% refund for cancellations 14-20 days before the trip
- No refund for cancellations less than 14 days before the trip
We also offer a flexible booking option with additional fee that allows for more generous cancellation terms.

Can you arrange custom itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized travel experiences tailored to your interests, preferences, and budget. Our travel experts will work with you to design a personalized itinerary that includes the destinations you want to visit, the activities you enjoy, and accommodations that match your comfort level. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll create the perfect travel plan for you.

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to visit India. E-Visa facilities are available for citizens of many countries, allowing you to apply online. The process typically takes 3-5 business days. We recommend applying for your visa at least one month before your trip. While we don't directly process visa applications, we're happy to provide guidance and the necessary documentation to support your visa application, such as confirmed itineraries and accommodation bookings.
